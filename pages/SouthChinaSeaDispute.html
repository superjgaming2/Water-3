Bodys of water around the world

The South China sea is a sea bordered by 4 ASEAN countries, Vietnam, Malaysia, Brunei, and The Philippines. The non-ASEAN countries that border it are The ROC and The PRC. There are currently 57 island bases, Brunei has 1, The ROC has 2, Malaysia has 6, The PRC has 7, The Philippines has 11, and Vietnam has 29. These bases are used to cement the claims of the occupier.
